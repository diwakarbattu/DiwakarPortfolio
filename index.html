About me

Hi, I am Battu
Professional UI/UX Designer and Front-End developer.

  • Connect with

    linkedin profile  

My work is to ease the communication between a human and a device by solving UX problems through beautiful, intuitive interfaces that consistently and rapidly increase product sales. I specialize in translating complex business needs into elegant and scalable design systems.

  • Around 12 years of professional experience as UI/UX Designer and Front end Developer With a banking, SaaS, and agro-tech domains
  • I bring a unique mix of design thinking, interaction design, and responsive front-end development.
  • Created user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and Adobe XD for enterprise web and mobile apps.
  • Translated complex business and user needs into UX solutions using Design Thinking and User-Centered Design (UCD) methodologies.
  • Worked closely with product owners, developers, and QA teams to align UI/UX with technical feasibility and business goals.
  • Converted static wireframes and prototypes into pixel-perfect, responsive UI using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and Angular.
  • Implemented interactive UI elements and animations while ensuring cross-browser and cross-device compatibility.
  • Integrated Material components and custom styles for a modern, accessible design experience.
  • Collaborated in Agile/Scrum environments; participated in daily standups, sprint planning, and design reviews.
  • Used tools like Git/GitHub, VS Code, and Jira for version control, code reviews, and task tracking.
